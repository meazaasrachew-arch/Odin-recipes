Lasagna

This page will show you how to make delicious lasagna.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta and Mozzarella cheese

steps

  1. Boil the pasta sheets until soft.
  2. Layer the sauce, pasta, and cheese in a dish.
  3. Bake in the oven until the cheese is golden.
